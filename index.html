Our Legacy

Founded in 1920, OPAL OUTFITTER began as a small bespoke shoemaking workshop in the heart of the city. What started as a passion for craftsmanship quickly evolved into a symbol of luxury and sophistication.

For over a century, we have been crafting exceptional footwear that combines traditional techniques with contemporary design. Each pair of shoes that leaves our atelier carries with it a legacy of excellence, attention to detail, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our master artisans, trained in the old-world methods of shoemaking, ensure that every stitch, every cut, and every polish meets the highest standards of luxury footwear. From royal families to modern connoisseurs, OPAL OUTFITTER has dressed the feet of discerning clients worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use in your shoes? +

We use only the finest full-grain leather, sourced from premium tanneries in Italy and Pakistan. Our soles are crafted from genuine leather or high-quality rubber, depending on the style. Each material is carefully selected for durability, comfort, and aesthetic appeal.

How do I care for my OPAL OUTFITTER shoes? +

We recommend using a soft cloth to remove dust after each wear. Apply our signature leather conditioner monthly to maintain suppleness. Store in the provided dust bag and use shoe trees to maintain shape. Avoid prolonged exposure to water and direct sunlight.

What is your delivery timeframe? +

Standard delivery within Pakistan takes 3-5 business days. For international orders, please allow 7-14 business days. Each pair is handcrafted and undergoes rigorous quality checks before shipping. Express delivery options are available at checkout.

Do you offer custom sizing? +

Yes! We offer bespoke sizing for clients with specific requirements. Please contact us via WhatsApp or email with your measurements, and our master craftsmen will create a pair tailored specifically to your feet. Additional charges may apply for custom work.
