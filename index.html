Our Heritage

For over a century, OPAL OUTFITTER has been the epitome of refined luxury and impeccable craftsmanship. Founded in 1920, our legacy began with a simple vision: to create garments that transcend trends and stand the test of time.

Each piece in our collection is a testament to the artistry of our master craftsmen, who blend traditional techniques with contemporary sensibilities. We believe that true luxury lies in the details – from the selection of the finest fabrics to the precision of every stitch.

Today, OPAL OUTFITTER continues to set the standard for sophisticated fashion, serving discerning individuals who appreciate the finer things in life. Our commitment to excellence remains as unwavering as it was a century ago.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes OPAL OUTFITTER different from other luxury brands? +

Since 1920, we have maintained an unwavering commitment to exceptional craftsmanship and timeless design. Each piece is handcrafted by master artisans using the finest materials sourced from around the world. Our dedication to quality ensures that every garment becomes a lasting investment piece.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes, we provide worldwide shipping with full insurance and customs clearance assistance. Orders are typically delivered within 5-7 business days via our premium courier partners. All shipments are discreetly packaged to ensure privacy and protection.

What is your return and exchange policy? +

We offer a comprehensive 30-day return policy for all unworn items in original condition. Custom and personalized pieces are non-returnable but can be altered. Our concierge team is available to assist with any fitting concerns.

Can I request custom alterations or bespoke designs? +

Absolutely. Our atelier offers bespoke services for discerning clients seeking unique pieces. Schedule a consultation with our design team to discuss your vision. Custom orders typically require 4-6 weeks for completion.
