Our Legacy

Founded in 1920, OPAL OUTFITTER began as a small bespoke shoemaking workshop in the heart of the city. What started as a passion for craftsmanship quickly evolved into a symbol of luxury and sophistication.

For over a century, we have been crafting exceptional footwear that combines traditional techniques with contemporary design. Each pair of shoes that leaves our atelier carries with it a legacy of excellence, attention to detail, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our master artisans, trained in the old-world methods of shoemaking, ensure that every stitch, every cut, and every polish meets the highest standards of luxury footwear. From royal families to modern connoisseurs, OPAL OUTFITTER has dressed the feet of discerning clients worldwide.
